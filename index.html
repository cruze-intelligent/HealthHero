How To Play

Mission guide

🗺️ Campaign flow

Start from the Dashboard, open a mission, finish its three stages, then read your mission report before moving to the next unlock.

🎮 Controls

Use touch, mouse, or keyboard. Every interactive element is a button, so Tab and Enter also work across the game. Action missions show a live goal counter and countdown timer.

⭐ Action stages

Watch the countdown, wait for GO, then hit only the correct targets before the timer runs out. If you miss the target threshold, use Retry Stage to replay instantly or Exit Mission to leave safely.

💾 Progress

Your unlocked missions, badges, best scores, reports, and checkpoint are saved locally so you can return without losing progress.
