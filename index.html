🦸 HEALTH HERO 🦸

Fight the Germs!

📖 How To Play

🎯 Your Mission: Become a Health Hero by defeating germs and learning about health!
🦠 Level 1: Germ Fighting

Click on the colorful germs to blast them away with soap bubbles!

  • Flu Virus (Red): Worth 10 points
  • Bacteria (Purple): Worth 15 points
  • Dirt Germ (Brown): Worth 20 points
Controls: Click each germ OR press Left/Right arrow keys to move around
💡 Health Tips

During gameplay, you'll see helpful health tips appear! These come from real health organizations and teach you about:

  • Hand washing and personal hygiene
  • Healthy eating and nutrition
  • Disease prevention
  • Good health habits
🧠 Level 2: Health Quiz

Answer 3 health questions to test your knowledge!

  • Each correct answer: +50 points
  • Choose the best answer from 4 options
  • Learn while you play!
🥗 Level 3: Healthy Meal Challenge

Select 3 healthy foods to make a balanced meal!

  • Choose at least 2 healthy foods for bonus points
  • Mix fruits, vegetables, proteins, and grains
  • Avoid junk foods!
⭐ Scoring & Tips

Killing Germs: Earn points for each germ (10-20 per germ)
Quiz Answers: 50 points per correct answer
Healthy Meals: Up to 100 bonus points

Challenge yourself: Try to get the highest score and become a true Health Hero!
