How To Play

Mission guide

Campaign flow

Move from Dashboard to Mission Map, complete three stages per mission, then review your report before the next unlock.

Controls

Use touch, mouse, or keyboard. Every interactive element is a button, so Tab and Enter work across the game.

Scoring

Passing a stage earns 100 points, perfect play adds 25, and action stages add energy and soap bonuses.

Progress

Your unlocked missions, badges, best scores, and current checkpoint are saved locally so you can resume later.
